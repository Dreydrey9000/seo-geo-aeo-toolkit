**Quick definitions (so we're on the same page):**

- **SEO (Search Engine Optimization):** making content rank in classic search results like Google's blue links — keywords, titles, and structure that match what people type into a search bar.
- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization):** making content show up *inside* AI-generated answers (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) — structured, quotable, and easy for an AI to pull from and trust.
- **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization):** making content the literal answer to a specific question someone asks an assistant — phrased so the engine can lift a clean, direct response straight from it.
