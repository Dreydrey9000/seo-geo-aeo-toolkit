FAQ

What should I automate before hiring?
Automate the repeatable, rules-based tasks first: scheduling, follow-ups, data entry, content repurposing, and reporting. These are the tasks AI systems handle well and that drain a founder's time.
Will AI systems make me work more?
No. Built right, AI systems do the same work with less of your time involved, so the promise is doing less, not cramming more into the day.
When does hiring actually make sense?
When the work needs human judgment, trust, or relationships that cannot be reduced to a repeatable process. Automate the rest first so the hire is smaller and later.
